SECTION: CREATING MEMORABLE EVENTS
====================================
So, many elements can create the perfect event, however, it needs to be done with signature artistry, an eye on design and a feeling of harmony that is true to your type of event. Whichever elements are used in your event they need to resonate your personality, your vision, and your chosen space.

We here at Hare Curated have transformed so many different spaces from reception halls, rooms, gardens, office spaces, warehouses, parking spaces, etc. If there is a possibility where more than two people can come together, we are keen, to transform those spaces. Either to enhance or to playoff what is already there or to transform into spaces that are a delightful feast for the senses for you and your guest. It does not matter if the brief is understated, relaxed, and paired back or all-out or anywhere in between. We make it your space and your signature event for that moment. We will always endeavour to set your event apart from the rest, because we care deeply about the artistic process, and in creating memories that will live with you and your guest long after the events are over.

Floral Design Styling Projects
------------------------------
Our founder and Owner, Erique Swart, has been a creative stylist for advertising campaigns in the magazine and film industry for almost 30 years. Shopping for Blooms
-------------------
We source various blooms from our vendors, wholesales, and auctions.

We even grow some of our own cut flower blooms namely almost seventy types of dahlias during the spring-summer dahlia growing season. Then we grow ranunculus, anemones and poppies during the autumn-winter and very early spring growing season. All the blooms we grow are specialist blooms.

We sell the source or requested blooms to our clients. But we also sell our own grown blooms to clients in bunches at retail price and wholesale to other florists. So, if and when you need let us know a day or two in advance and we will get you what you need. We also sell tubers, corms and seedlings for these varieties.
