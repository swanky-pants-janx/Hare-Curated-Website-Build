EVENT 4: FEED THE SOIL — A HANDS-ON WORKSHOP
==============================================
Date: 1 March 2025
Time: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM
Location: South Africa
Price: R500 per person (limited to 15 participants)
CTA: Book Now / Reserve Your Seat Today!

Description:
Celebrate the start of autumn by nurturing the very heart of your garden — its soil. Join us for "Feed the Soil," a four-hour interactive workshop designed to help you understand the fundamentals of building and maintaining a vibrant, nutrient-rich earth. We'll explore natural and organic fertiliser inputs, discover the role of soil amendments, and dive into the art of composting. Together, we'll use the lingering warmth before the cooler days ahead to set the stage for thriving soil biology that will give your spring garden a significant head start.

WHAT'S INCLUDED:
- Engaging discussions on organic fertiliser inputs
- Expert guidance on selecting and applying soil amendments
- Hands-on composting demonstrations
- A complimentary goody bag featuring sample sizes of Hare Curated's natural and organic inputs

Space is limited to just 15 participants to ensure personalized attention and guidance. Secure your spot now for only R500 and equip yourself with the knowledge to cultivate healthy soil and abundant harvests in the seasons to come.
