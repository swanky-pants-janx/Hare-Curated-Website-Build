Our Founder

Erique Swart

Creative Director & Owner

Erique Swart has been a creative stylist for advertising campaigns in the magazine and film industry for almost 30 years. He has collaborated on television series and movies for the likes of Netflix, Amazon Prime, and Ochre Media, and spent many years creating iconic commercials with some of the top names in the commercial industry.

It is this deep well of creative experience that he brings to every floral design moment and event he touches. He grows nearly seventy varieties of dahlias during the spring-summer season, and ranunculus, anemones, and poppies during the cooler months — each one a specialist bloom cultivated with care.

One of his greatest accomplishments is forming wonderful friendships with both clients and vendors over the years — because for Erique, every event is personal.
